Common Questions

What are the best foods for a healthy diet?

A healthy diet includes fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

How much exercise do I need daily?

Experts recommend at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days of the week.

Why is sleep important for health?

Good sleep helps improve focus, boosts immunity, and promotes overall well-being.

How can I reduce stress naturally?

Practice meditation, exercise regularly, eat a balanced diet, and maintain a positive mindset.
